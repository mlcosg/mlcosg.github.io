Correlations of UFO Sightings by Shape

There appears to be a moderate negative relationship between reports of "geometric" objects vs "light" objects (-.61). This means that over time (over the years) when reports of geometric objects are made, there is a moderate negative relationship on reports of light objects (or vice versa). Better tests to use on this data would be similarity tests, Jaccard coefficient, among others. The internet is divided on whether you should/could do a Pearson correlation on binary variables, some say yes, others say no. You would also want the p-values of the correlation coefficients reported, but I'm fairly new to programming and couldn't figure out the reporting of p-values in time for the due date on the project. :/
