UFO Sightings by Shape by Weekday

This chart shows there is a decrease in duration of sightings from Thurs-Fri, followed by an increase in duration of sightings from Friday to Saturday, and again followed by decrease in duration Sat-Sun. This trend might be explained by people tending to be up later on the weekends and therefore have more opportunity to observe the night/dark sky.
